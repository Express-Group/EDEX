A single career for life in a stable workforce is now a fallacy. The future is rapidly changing but holds exciting possibilities in the new work order.

The New Indian Express has collaborated with Global Education Solutions, to prepare school students thrive in this transforming world, by building a valuable skill - how to make career decisions, lifelong!

This globally unique career decision making tool, provides an indication of a student's preferred working styles, natural strengths and abilities, their personality types and interests. And then helps them make occupational, educational and subject choices based on their compatibility to the dynamic job market.
