The New Indian Express has collaborated with the Global Education Solutions and the Indian Career Education and Development Council to inspire every young person, irrespective of their socio economic background, abilities and resources, to understand themselves, explore the changing world of opportunities and find their fit, and plan a happy and successful career.

This easy to use career decision making tool is based on scientifically proven theories which provide an indication of a student's preferred working styles, natural strengths and abilities, their personality types and interests. It helps them explore options and make occupational, educational and subject choices that suit them best, based on their compatibility and the changing job market. This can be completed at school or at home using the globally unique personalised digital platform.

Students

  • If you have decided on what to do when you finish school, gauge whether you are on the right track or explore new possibilities that you didn’t know.
  • If you have diverse interests and are not sure which direction to take, simulate can help you work out innovative career options.
  • If you have absolutely no clue, relax! Simulate can help you identify opportunities that are aligned to your strengths, innate talents, abilities, interests and importantly your fit in the job market.
  • Helps you identify the potential of your child, especially when their career interests keep changing and you are not sure what is right for them and why.
  • Find out about occupations and study specialisations in the new work order, that you may not be familiar with.

Parents

Teachers/ Counsellors

  • Advise students on subject combinations that gives them the best foundation for their evolving future career journey
  • Recommend further study options not just based on academic results and admission criteria, or where alumni studied, but on graduate outcomes.
  • Empower the school community with a comprehensive, unbiased, trusted career coaching tool to guide students with confidence.
  • Enable all your higher secondary students, irrespective of their academic achievement, find the right fit - something that they enjoy, are good at, is what employers need and are prepared to pay for.
